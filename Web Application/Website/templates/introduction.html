Introduction

    How is the Decision Process working?:

  • At first you are conducting the pairwise comparison of the criteria.
  • Then the Consistency Ratio is calculated. A lower consistency ratio indicates greater reliability and
    confidence in the decision-making process, while a higher ratio suggests a need for reconsideration or
    refinement of the pairwise comparisons to enhance the robustness of the AHP model.
  • Afterwards you can see the criteria weight and sensitivity analysis.
  • Lastly the Alternatives (Technologies) will be conmpared and the most suitable ILT will be recommended.
  • At the end you can download the report of the calculation.
    Which criteria are considered?:

  •      - Accuracy
  •      - Cost
  •      - Robustness
  •      - Coverage Area
  •      - Response Time
  •      - Power Consumption
  •      - Scalability
    Which alternatives are compared?:

  •      - WIFI
  •      - RFID
  •      - Bluetooth
  •      - UWB
  •      - VLC
  •      - Zigbee
  •      - Ultrasound
    How is the result calculated?:

  • The result will be calculated based on the AHP calculation method.
  • Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) is a decision-making methodology that systematically
    breaks down complex problems into a hierarchy of criteria and alternatives, facilitating
    pairwise comparisons to quantify preferences and derive a consistent overall ranking or choice.
